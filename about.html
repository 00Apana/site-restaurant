Our Story: A Commitment to Quality

The Meridian Table was founded in year 2012 with a simple vision: to bring sophisticated, seasonally-inspired dining to our neighborhood without the formality. We believe that exceptional food is the foundation of every great community gathering.

Our Culinary Philosophy

Source Local

We work directly with local farmers and artisans within a 50-mile radius to ensure the freshest, highest-quality ingredients for every dish we serve.

Timeless Technique

Our kitchen blends classic French techniques with modern presentation, creating dishes that are both comforting and unexpectedly elegant.

Sustainable Dining

From reducing waste to energy-efficient practices, sustainability is at the heart of our operations, guaranteeing a better tomorrow for all.

Meet the Chef

Chef Alex Chen, Head Chef & Founder

Chef Alex Chen trained at the Culinary Institute of Food Science and has spent two decades refining his approach in some of the world's most acclaimed kitchens. His personal touch is evident in every menu item, combining global flavors with a deep reverence for regional traditions.

Our greatest joy is seeing a table of guests truly connect over a meal we prepared with passion.
